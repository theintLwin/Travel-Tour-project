Welcome From Taunggyi-Inlay-PinTaYa-Kalaw

Taunggyi is the capital of shan state and largest city of shan state.Myanmar and lies on the Thazi-Kyaingtong road at an elevation of 4712 feet.It has plenty of people group and delicious food too.The traditional dresses of shan are the most beautiful. Taunggyi hot air balloon festival, the signature of this place is TazaungTine festiavl.Though all people from different regions of Myanmar participate in their different ways.Thaunggyi festiavl is the most prominent.
Inly Lake , the heart of the Shan State and the 2nd largest freshwater lake in Myanmar. It is located in the Nyaungshwe Township of Taunggyi.Consists mainly of Intha, with a mix of other Shan nation, Pa-O (Taungthu), Danu, Danaw and other Bama ethnicities.Food - local dish ,we call hta-min-chin , sticky rice mixwelled with Fish or Potato.Hpaung-Taw-Oo Pagoda festival is held near Thadingyut festival(lighting festival) and lasts approximately for 18 days.
Pindaya is a town in the Shan State of Burma. It is located in the west of the state in Pindaya Township in Taunggyi District. Mainly famous for its limestone caves called Pindaya Caves where thousands of Buddha images have been consecrated for worship over the centuries.
Kalaw is located near Taunggyi and it is famous for its weather because it is hill town. All the view of this town make u 100% relax.
